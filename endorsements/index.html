Voices of Support from American Exporters,
Farmers, and Businesses

Across the country, a diverse coalition of American farmers, businesses, ranchers, and exporters are speaking out about the urgent need to pass the Trans-Pacific Partnership. These groups recognize that cutting over 18,000 taxes that other countries place on Made-in-America exports will help them grow and support their workers. They also recognize that America must step up to define the rules of the road on trade so that our international competitors don't define the rules of the road in a way that undercuts our exports. Below is a look at just some of the support the TPP is getting from a variety of industries that help support well-paying American jobs.
